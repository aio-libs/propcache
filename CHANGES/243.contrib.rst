Introduced an in-tree :file:`tox.ini` and integrated
``tox-dev/workflow``'s :file:`reusable-tox.yml` into CI/CD. The
``build-pure-python-dists`` job is replaced by a ``build`` job
that builds the sdist and pure-python wheel through the
``build-dists`` tox env. A new ``lint`` matrix runs
``pre-commit`` and ``spellcheck-docs`` through ``tox-dev/workflow``,
``mypy`` coverage is uploaded to Coveralls from a
``post-tox-job`` hook, and a separate ``metadata-validation`` job
runs ``twine check`` against every built dist (the pure-python
wheel plus every ``cibuildwheel`` matrix output). The legacy
``Linter`` reusable workflow has been removed
-- by :user:`bdraco`.
