Introduced an in-tree :file:`tox.ini` and integrated
``tox-dev/workflow``'s :file:`reusable-tox.yml` into CI/CD. The
``build-pure-python-dists`` job is replaced by a ``build`` job
that builds the sdist and pure-python wheel through the
``build-dists`` tox env, and a new ``lint`` job runs
``pre-commit`` through ``tox-dev/workflow``. The existing
``Linter`` workflow continues to run as ``retro-lint`` to keep
``mypy``, ``twine check`` and ``spellcheck-docs`` coverage during
the transition
-- by :user:`bdraco`.
